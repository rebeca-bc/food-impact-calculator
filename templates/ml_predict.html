Training Details

  • Training Data: 43 foods with complete LCA data
  • Features: 7 supply chain stages (land use, farm, transport, etc.)
  • Split: 80% training, 20% testing
  • Cross-Validation: 5-fold to ensure robustness
  • Feature Importance: Farm (48%) > Land Use (37%) > Processing (5%)

How It Works

  1. You input the 7 supply chain emission values
  2. Model analyzes patterns learned from 43 real foods
  3. Ensemble of 100 trees vote on the prediction
  4. Average prediction is returned with high confidence

Note: This model works best for foods with similar production patterns to those in our training data. Unusual foods may have less accurate predictions.
